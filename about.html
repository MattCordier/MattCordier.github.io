Matt Cordier

Musician/Developer/Educator

I'm a web developer and a drummer with decades of performing and teaching experience.

I feel, as you may, that what defines us goes well beyond what we do for money. I work a job but I am not my job. The real me is recreated everyday through my habits, intention, and focus.

This site is an exploration of one central and urgent question...
How does one maintain pursuit of that which lights the fire in your heart while tending to the pragmatic and mundane of everyday living?

It's my intention that the articles, interviews, and ideas here provide you with kindling to help keep your fire burning hot and bright.
