On A Personal Note…

I moved to Milwaukee a few years ago and left behind an incredible culture of music and friendship in Chicago.

While the Windy City is only a couple hours away, I couldn't spend enough time there to be part of "the scene."

It took a while to get my bearing but MKE has started opening up to me. I was happy to find a thriving culture of improvisers, not to mention the non-stop music events during the summertime.

I even started playing with a killer group of horn players in the Hot & Dirty Brass Band!

But, I still missed my students back in Chicago. I missed sharing my experience and guiding people through their own drumming journey.

It was time to start teaching again.
